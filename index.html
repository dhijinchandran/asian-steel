Our Services

We Provide Services

TMT steel

TMT steel is a type of steel used in construction that stands for Thermo-Mechanically Treated steel. It is made by subjecting mild steel bars to a series of heating and cooling processes that alter its properties, making it stronger and more durable.

GI and GP pipes

GI and GP pipes is available in a variety of thicknesses, ranging from very thin sheets to thick plates. It can be manufactured from different grades of steel, including carbon steel, alloy steel, and stainless steel, depending on the intended use and application

Cement

Cement is a powdery substance made from a mixture of limestone, clay, and other minerals. It is one of the most widely used building materials in the world and is a critical component in the construction industry.

Flat and Angles

The flat and angled steel bars are essential components used in a wide range of industries and applications. Constructed from high-quality steel, these bars are known for their exceptional strength, durability, and versatility. They are designed to provide structural stability and support in various construction, manufacturing, and engineering projects.

Polycarbon

Polycarbonate (PC) is a thermoplastic material that is widely used in various industries due to its excellent properties, including high impact resistance, clarity, and heat resistance. It is commonly used in applications that require a high level of strength, durability, and optical clarity.

Net

Home safety nets are typically easy to install and can be customized to fit the specific needs of the home. They are available in different sizes, shapes, and colors, and can be installed using hooks, cables, or other anchoring systems.

Roofing sheet

Roofing sheets are thin, flat sheets of various materials that are used to cover roofs. They are commonly made from materials such as metal, plastic, or asphalt, and come in different shapes and sizes depending on the type of roof they are intended to cover.

I Beams

I Beams, also known as L-shaped steel or angle iron, is a type of steel that has an L-shaped cross-section. It is commonly used in construction, manufacturing, and engineering applications for providing structural support and reinforcement.
